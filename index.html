PROCESS

1.

Design

The first stage of architecture is the design and conceptualization phase. This is where ideas take shape, and creativity flourishes. Our team of skilled architects and designers collaborate with you to understand your vision, needs, and aspirations.

2.

Execution

Once the design and concept are finalized, we move on to the development stage. This is where the architectural plans come to fruition. Our team of experts works meticulously to refine the design, create detailed construction documents, and obtain necessary permits and approvals.

3.

Completion

Once construction is finished, the architect & professionals, conducts thorough inspections of the building to ensure that it meets all code compliance standards. This includes examining structural integrity, mechanical & electrical components, fire safety measures, and overall functionality.
